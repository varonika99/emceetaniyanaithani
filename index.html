About Me

Taniya Naithani is not just an emcee; she’s an experience. With over a decade of professional anchoring under her belt, she has carved a niche for herself in the world of live entertainment, events, and celebrations. Her journey began with an unshakable love for the stage — from inter-school competitions to major corporate galas. Over time, that spark evolved into a blazing passion that has taken her across the country and beyond, commanding audiences of thousands with grace, spontaneity, and unmistakable charisma.

With a degree in mass communication and years of hands-on experience, Taniya brings the rare combination of eloquence and empathy. She doesn’t just speak to the audience — she connects with them. Whether it’s a high-profile wedding, a Fortune 500 corporate summit, a fashion runway show, or a cultural festival, Taniya adapts effortlessly to the mood and theme of the event.

Her strengths lie in her improvisational skills, clear diction, multilingual fluency, and natural crowd engagement. Audiences often describe her as energetic, elegant, and emotionally intelligent. Organizers praise her professionalism, punctuality, and ability to handle high-pressure situations with a calm and collected demeanor.

Taniya’s reach extends beyond the stage. With a strong digital presence on YouTube and Instagram, she keeps her followers engaged with event snippets, behind-the-scenes moments, hosting tips, and inspirational content for aspiring anchors. Her fans often highlight how she strikes the perfect balance between glamour and relatability — a rare trait in the world of entertainment.

She has hosted over 1000+ events, including international conferences, luxury weddings, product launches, television shows, college fests, and government functions. Her impressive client list includes major brands such as Samsung, Maruti Suzuki, HDFC, Amazon, and government tourism boards. Regardless of the scale or scope of the event, Taniya brings the same energy and enthusiasm to each occasion.

Her vision is simple yet powerful: to elevate every event she hosts into a memory that lingers. She believes that the anchor sets the tone — and with her around, you can expect nothing short of spectacular.

So, if you're looking for someone who can breathe life into your event, seamlessly stitch together segments, engage with the audience like a trusted friend, and carry your event with poise and precision — you’ve found your emcee.

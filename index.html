About Me

With over a decade of experience engaging live audiences across India, I, Taniya Naithani, specialize in transforming events into unforgettable memories. As a premium emcee, I bring elegance, charm, and energy to every stage I step onto. Whether it’s a destination wedding, a high-profile corporate gala, or an entertainment extravaganza, I tailor my approach to your unique event goals. Fluent in Hindi, English, and the universal language of joy, I’ve hosted 500+ events across metros and tier-2 cities alike. My stage presence is magnetic, professional, and always guest-focused.

My clients include industry-leading brands, production houses, wedding planners, and private clients who seek a touch of class and flair in their events. Every show I host is meticulously planned, with attention to flow, tone, audience engagement, and lasting impact. I’m not just an anchor—I am your event’s storyteller, energizer, and curator of unforgettable moments.

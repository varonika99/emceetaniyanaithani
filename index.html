About Me

Taniya Naithani is a celebrated emcee and anchor known for her electrifying stage presence and ability to engage audiences of all types. From glamorous weddings to high-energy corporate events and cultural galas, she has hosted hundreds of shows across India and internationally.

With a natural flair for storytelling and improvisation, Taniya brings a vibrant charm and unmatched professionalism to every event she hosts. Whether it’s anchoring a fashion show, product launch, or festive celebration, she ensures every moment is memorable for the audience and the organizers alike.
